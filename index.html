Lifestyle as medicine: The six pillars

Nutrition

Evidence supports the use of a whole food, plant-predominant diet to prevent, treat and reverse chronic illness.

Physical activity

Regular physical activity is key to managing weight, improving mental health, and reducing chronic disease risk.

Restorative sleep

Consistent, quality sleep is crucial for brain function and physical health.

Stress management

Effective stress management techniques help reduce inflammation and support immunity.

Social connection

Healthy relationships support emotional well-being and longevity.

Substance abuse

Reducing or eliminating addictive substances leads to improved overall health.
